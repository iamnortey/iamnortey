## What I Build

I build tools that let operators run their platforms from natural language instead of dashboards. The focus is on AI infrastructure that connects thinking tools (Claude, ChatGPT) to systems of record (Circle, Kajabi, and others) — so operators can inspect, diagnose, and act without switching contexts.

## How I Work

- **Data model first, then features.** The schema is the foundation.
- **Runbooks, decision logs, and audit trails are first-class deliverables** — not afterthoughts.
- **Phase-gated development.** No Phase 2 until Phase 1 passes all quality gates.
- **Building for places where trust is low and WhatsApp is the platform.**
