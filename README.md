## How I Work

- **Data model first, then features.** The schema is the foundation.
- **Runbooks, decision logs, and audit trails are first-class deliverables** — not afterthoughts.
- **Phase-gated development.** No Phase 2 until Phase 1 passes all quality gates.
- **Building for places where trust is low and WhatsApp is the platform.**
