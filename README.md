## What I build

I build tools and systems that help operators work more clearly and reliably. My current focus spans three areas: AI interfaces for SaaS platforms, education data infrastructure for Ghanaian curriculum and exam intelligence, and AWS-native training labs for AI CloudOps and security. The throughline is the same across all of it — reduce friction, make systems easier to operate, and design for evidence over claims.

## How I work

- **Data model first, then features.** The schema is the foundation.
- **Runbooks, decision logs, and audit trails are first-class deliverables** — not afterthoughts.
- **Phase-gated development.** No Phase 2 until Phase 1 passes all quality gates.
- **Governance before deployment.** Guardrails precede access, especially in AWS and education contexts.
- **Building for places where trust is low and WhatsApp is the platform.**
